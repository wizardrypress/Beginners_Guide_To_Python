# English Edition Selector

Choose the folder that matches the edition printed on your book.

| Book edition | Folder |
|---|---|
| Third Edition / 2026 | [`Third Edition/`](Third%20Edition/) |
| 2025 | [`2025/`](2025/) |
| 2024 | [`2024/`](2024/) |

If your book says **Third Edition** or **2026**, use [`Third Edition/`](Third%20Edition/).

The [`2026/`](2026/) folder is only a redirect folder kept for readers who look for the edition by year.
